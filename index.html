What We Do

Mentorship Cohorts

Annual mentorship cohorts spanning eight months with structured virtual and in-person sessions covering grant writing, public speaking, career planning, and CV development.

Collaborative Projects

MedXMentor partners with organizations like Mejo Africa and Afrihealth allowing mentees to work on collaborative projects like research proposals with mentor guidance.

Campus Ambassadors

Trained campus ambassadors lead local chapters, organize events, bring students together, and promote a culture of mentorship for accessibility and sustainability.
