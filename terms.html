Terms & Conditions

These terms explain how MedXMentor collects and uses information to support your mentoring experience, including the Virtual Mentor feature.

Virtual Mentor Conversations

When you use the Virtual Mentor, we store your introduction, hobbies, interests, goals, and chat history to personalize your guidance. This information helps the mentor remember your context across sessions.

Data Usage

  • Your profile details are used to tailor mentoring advice.
  • Conversation history is saved to provide continuity in future chats.
  • We do not share your data outside MedXMentor without your consent.

Security & Access

Access to your data is tied to your MedXMentor account. If you log out, your conversation history remains available for future sessions once you log back in.

We may update these terms to improve the service. Continued use of the Virtual Mentor after updates means you accept the revised terms.
