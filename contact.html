How to Get Involved

Become a Mentee

Are you a health science student looking for guidance? Join our mentorship program to connect with experienced professionals and accelerate your career development.

  • Access to experienced mentors
  • Skills development workshops
  • Career planning support
  • Research collaboration opportunities

Become a Mentor

Share your expertise and experience with the next generation of health professionals. Make a lasting impact on students' careers and contribute to Africa's healthcare future.

  • Flexible mentoring schedule
  • Structured mentoring framework
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Network with other mentors

Campus Ambassador

Lead the mentorship movement on your campus. Organize events, recruit students, and build a culture of mentorship within your university community.

  • Leadership development
  • Event organization training
  • Networking opportunities
  • Recognition and certification

Subscribe to our newsletter for updates on the latest events, calls, and mentorship articles.
