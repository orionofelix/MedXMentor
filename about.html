MedXMentor Mentorship Program

MedXMentor is a hybrid mentorship program launched on 25th February 2022, targeting future health science professionals who are the future backbone of Africa's health sector. Since launch we have made significant strides in achieving our objectives.

Pilot Cohort Completion

Successfully completed our pilot cohort with 22 mentors and 45 mentees, demonstrating the effectiveness of our mentorship model.

International Partnerships

Established partnerships with medical institutions in over 8 countries in Africa, including Ethiopia, Uganda, Burundi, Rwanda, Nigeria, Sierra Leone, Tanzania, and Kenya.

Research-Based Results

Conducted a baseline survey on access to and relevance of mentorship amongst Health professional students in Uganda, extending to Africa.

Our Vision

To be a hub of excellence in producing well-rounded future health professionals to be strong pillars of our health sectors.

Our Mission

We Journey with You

MedXMentor is committed to providing future health professionals with knowledge, skills, confidence, motivation, experiences, and life lessons through a mentoring program.

Our Objectives

01

Functionalize Mentorship Programs

To functionalize working student-led hybrid mentorship programs to foster leadership, community engagement, and professional career development.

02

Connect Students with Mentors

To help young health professional students find mentors and actively engage with mentors in professional development, career choices, and skills development.

03

Build Mentor Community

To create an engaging community of mentors for health professional students.

04

Spread Mentorship Culture

To spread the idea of mentorship among students and youth pursuing careers in the health sector through engaging in meaningful discussions about mentorship.
